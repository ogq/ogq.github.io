Backgrounds HD
30M+ downloaded from 200 countries
Backgrounds HD is a free smartphone app providing beautiful, curated images for lock screen & backgrounds. Launched in Jul 2011, Backgrounds HD ranked the number 1 for 2 weeks among free apps in Google play store. Now Backgrounds HD is beloved from 30,000,000+ downloads(Jan 2014) through 39 countries as an emotional platform for people to connect.
TEDAir
Enjoy TED talks with 80+ languages
TED stands for Technology, Entertainment and Design under the slogan - "Ideas worth spreading". TEDAir for Android, the first product of OGQ team, has been made to enable users to find and share TED speech easily. To achieve the mission that TED and OGQ share, TEDAir provides subtitles in over 90 languages with autotranslation.
